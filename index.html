About me

You are probably wondering, how come a double bass player get into programming. When I was a child, I used to play with my dad's 486 PC. I fiddled around with Turbo Pascal, trying to recreate my favourite games like Commander Keen. Later, in high school, a turned to music, and after graduating I started to work as a double bassist in different orchestras. In 2020, while the Covid-19 epidemic ravaged through the world, I was looking for something to study during the quarantine. By one of my friends recommendation, I tried the freeCodeCamp website - and that's how it all started...
